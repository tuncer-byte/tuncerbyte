## 1. Google: GenAI Learning Path

Prepared by Google Cloud, this learning path covers everything from large language models to image generation. Completers receive an official Google badge.

**What you'll learn:**
- How large language models work
- Prompt design and optimization
- Image generation models
- Google Cloud AI services

## 2. Microsoft: AI Fundamentals

A globally recognized starting point that covers AI services on Azure and fundamental machine learning concepts. Integrates with Microsoft's global certification ecosystem.

**What you'll learn:**
- Machine learning fundamentals
- Computer vision and natural language processing
- Azure AI services
- Responsible AI principles

## 3. Harvard: CS50 AI

Harvard's legendary CS50 series applied to artificial intelligence. Teaches modern AI algorithms in depth using Python. Academic quality, free access.

**What you'll learn:**
- Search algorithms and optimization
- Machine learning and neural networks
- Natural language processing
- Practical Python implementations

## 4. Elements of AI

A joint course by the University of Helsinki and Reaktor. Explains AI's logic and its place in society without drowning you in academic jargon. Available in multiple languages including Turkish.

**What you'll learn:**
- What AI is — and what it isn't
- The core logic behind machine learning
- AI's impact on daily life and society
- Ethics and future scenarios

## 5. IBM: AI for Everyone

No technical background required. Covers AI ethics, data science, and real-world business applications. Available free on Coursera via the **"Audit"** option.

**What you'll learn:**
- AI strategy and business applications
- Data science fundamentals
- AI ethics and bias
- Using AI in teams and organizations

> **Note:** On the Coursera page, click "Audit" instead of "Enroll for Free." This gives full access to all content at no cost.
